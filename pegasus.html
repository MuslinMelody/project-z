The Pegasus Device

An MLP fanfiction called Pegasus Device inspired three songs: Rainbow Factory by WoodenToaster, Awoken by WoodenToaster and H8_Seed, and Pegasus Device by SlyphStorm. The fanfiction itself wasn't extremely bad (I wouldn't recommend it), but the end wasn't very satisfying. In essence, rainbows are made by Cloudsdale Weather Corporation in its rainbow factory. A component of this factory, called the pegasus device, extracts the color from and kills the children provided to it. These children are those who couldn't pass their flight test.

Due to an unfortunate incident in which the original lyrics of the song Pegasus Device include the words 'purple' and 'orange' on the same line that would make any non-brony automatically die, I replaced the line.

Someone who sounds like Rainbow Dash has covery things of all three: Rainbow Factory, Awoken, and Pegasus Device.
