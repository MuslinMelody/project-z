Pink Side of the Moon

A concept album by 4everfreebrony. It describes a retreat from life.

It contains a couple of monologues that are uncomfortable to listen to. The first one is reminiscent of the burger king foot lettuce thing. The monologues, and a few minor things, are the only reasons I wouldn't recommend this album, especially to non-bronies. Most of the songs themselves are excellent.

The link I provided was a re-mastered version of 4everbrony's original album. The most significant differences are the redone Nebular Skater and the new song Right Here.

Popular favorites in this album include Without Me, Reality, and Right Here.
